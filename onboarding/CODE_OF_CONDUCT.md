# Spendly – Code of Conduct

All contributors must follow these rules:

---

## 1. Respect
- No harassment
- No discrimination
- Be polite and professional

---

## 2. Communication
- Reply to messages within 24 hours
- Maintain clarity and cooperation

---

## 3. Ethics
- No stealing code
- No leaking private repository
- No sharing internal discussions

---

## 4. Removal
Violation may lead to immediate removal from the program.
